Dear Reader,

Hellooo~ I'm Melisa and this is my digital portfolio. I am from NYC and have had an affinity for art for as long as I can remember. I love drawing and working with various mediums, both traditional and non-traditional. I create art for myself and those alike, and believe that consistency is a trustworthy expression of one's true self.

While I enjoy painting, sketching, and all things alike, I love exploring more unconventional mediums too such as nail art, cake decoration, soft structures, and 3D modeling.

I think art is incredibly important as it surrounds us in our daily lives and can heavily influence our feelings and thoughts. I hope I can help more people come to this understanding through my time.

Love,
Melisa
